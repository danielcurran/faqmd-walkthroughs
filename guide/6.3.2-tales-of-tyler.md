There are some weapons and armour here, but none of it is worth buying as it will all be available with much more equipment at the next town. Instead, head over to the building directly north of the Inn to the right, and enter. You'll notice the same music that played when you first met Grandfather Dorin all the way back in Tonoe. This is not surprising, as you are about to meet his Dezolisian counter-part, whose name is Gyuna. Go to the bar and speak to Gyuna. He will see you are friends of Raja, and will answer some questions for you. Reply 'no' to knowledge about the snowstorm, the Garuberk Tower and Raja, but reply 'yes' to finding out about the whereabouts of the space ship. He will tell you that the ship will be found underground in Tyler, and that examining the grave will open up the path. With this unusual information, head out of the building and buy whatever supplies you may need. Dezolis is home to some tough enemies, so it might be a good idea to stock up a little more than usual. Rest at the Inn to recover the party, then leave Ryuon. Continue north and then head around the mountains to the west and you should soon reach Tyler. Walk on in.

First things first, sell all the old equipment you can. Also, sell any old curative items that have no real use any more. Once you have done this, you need to buy some new equipment. Purchase two SILV-MAILs for Chaz and Rika, a SILV-MANTL and a SILV-CIRCLT for Rune, a SILV-HELM for Chaz, a SILV-CROWN for Rika, and four SILVSHIELDs (two each for Rune and Raja). This shopping bill will come to 74300 Mesetas, which you may find a little more than you can afford. If so, buy as much as you can (leaving the SILVSHIELDs till last), then equip the items and rest at the Inn. Just to the north of the entrance to Tyler, head west and then ascend the short flight of steps to the north. In the centre of this little hill is a headstone. Examine this. "Here lies the great leader Tyler... Touch us... Listen to our voice... Entrust that body to us..." It is Tyler's grave, so Chaz inspects the stone as Gyuna suggested. While examining the inscription again the plate moves, so Chaz pushes it and the gravestone splits apart revealing a staircase below. Save, then descend.
