### 12.2.8. Light

**Skills:** EFESS, ST. FIRE · **Combos:** GrandCross · **Weapons:** PSYCO-WAND, SILVR-TUSK, ELSYDEON · **Effective Against:** All Darkspawn and Evil Beings (not Spearmen or Mages), · **Ineffective Against:** All enemies that are not Darkspawn or Evil Beings,

Light abilities make use of celestial energies and the power of The Great Light to cast down denizens of the Darkness. They are in the form of beams or waves of pure holy light. Techniques: - Dark Force, Profound Darkness Spearmen, Mages
